    Instructions to Use Gale On-line Courses at Hawaii Public Libraries

    1. Obtain a free library card, e.g., from the front desk of the Hilo Public Library (HPL).
    2. Log-on to the Hawaii Library website: Hawaii Library
    3. Scroll down about 1/3 of the way to the end of the opening page to a section called: Do more (than just read). (Hint: On the webpage, press the control key (Ctrl) and the 'f' key at the same time. When the search (find) box opens at the bottom of the page, type: Do more The page should then jump to the section you want.)
    4. Below this section, the center column is titled: Learn something new. Click on Gale Courses, and then chose the category of courses you wish to attend (e.g., College Readiness).
    5. Click on the course in which you are interested (e.g., Math). When the course's webpage appears, read the Detail and Syllabus sections. Then click Enroll Now.
    6. Note the course rules at the bottom of the homepage: You must "attend" a class twice in the first 13 days to remain enrolled.

      Instructions to Use Mango Languages at Hawaii Public Libraries

      1. Obtain a free library card, e.g., from the front desk of the Hilo Public Library (HPL).
      2. Log-on to the Hawaii Library website: Hawaii Library
      3. Scroll down about 1/3 of the way to the end of the opening page to a section called: Do more (than just read). (Hint: On the webpage, press the control key (Ctrl) and the 'f' key at the same time. When the search (find) box opens at the bottom of the page, type: Do more The page should then jump to the section you want.)
      4. Below this section, the center column is titled: Learn something new. Click on Mango languages. You will be given two choices: Log-in to the Mango site, or choose Guest Access. As a new visitor to the Mango language site, you may want to click the guest access button.
      5. When the Mango languages page opens, click on All languages. Then click on your choice, e.g., Chinese, Mandarin. When the new page is displayed, choose Mango Conversations. A new page will display and load the conversations. To begin hearing and repeating the English conversation with Chinese (or whatever your choice) translation, click on Unit 1, or Take Placement Test. Then, just follow the instructions and choices that follow on subsequent pages.
